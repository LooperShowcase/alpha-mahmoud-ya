what are crows

is a cosmopolitan family of oscine passerine birds that contains the crows, ravens, rooks, magpies, jackdaws, jays, treepies, choughs, and nutcrackers. In colloquial English, they are known as the crow family or corvids.

crows avrage life

Crows are known to live 7-8 years in the wild. Their lifespan is dependent on their ability to evade predators and to get food. There are some crows that are known to live up to 20 years.
